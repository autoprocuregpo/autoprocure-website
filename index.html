Why AutoProcure

More than just lower prices

Most dealerships overspend on indirect categories not because they aren't diligent — but because negotiating supplier contracts isn't their core business. AutoProcure changes that equation.

Significant cost savings

By aggregating spend across our dealer network, we unlock pricing and contract terms no single dealership could negotiate alone — with average savings of 15–30%+ across participating categories.

Vetted, high-quality suppliers

We identify, evaluate, and onboard best-in-class vendors in each category. You're not just paying less — you're working with better, more reliable partners from day one.

Guaranteed service levels

Our contracts include SLA commitments that protect your operations — agreed delivery windows, reliability standards, and clear recourse when vendors fall short.

An extension of your team

Think of AutoProcure as an outsourced procurement and finance function. We handle vendor management, benchmarking, and contract renewals so your team stays focused on selling and servicing cars.
