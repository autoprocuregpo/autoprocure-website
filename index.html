Why AutoProcure

More than just lower prices

Most dealerships overspend on indirect categories not because they aren't diligent — but because negotiating supplier contracts isn't their core business. AutoProcure changes that equation.

Committed supplier programs — not vendor-neutral advice. Unlike procurement consultants who evaluate a rotating pool of suppliers on your behalf, AutoProcure negotiates and commits to specific, best-in-class supplier programs in each category. You're not getting a shortlist — you're getting the deal we've already locked in on behalf of the entire network. We're accountable for the outcome, not just the process.

Significant cost savings

200+ dealers in the network creates purchasing scale no single rooftop can replicate — unlocking pricing and contract terms that typically deliver 15–30%+ savings across participating categories.

Committed, best-in-class suppliers

We don't hand you a list of vendors — we've already selected and locked in the best provider in each category. Better suppliers, better reliability, and SLAs we're on the hook for.

Guaranteed service levels

Every program includes SLA commitments — agreed delivery windows, reliability standards, and clear recourse when a supplier falls short.

An extension of your team

We handle vendor management, benchmarking, and contract renewals — so your team stays focused on selling and servicing cars, not chasing suppliers.
