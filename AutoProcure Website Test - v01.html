Your dealership's purchasing power, multiplied

What we cover

Spend categories we manage for you

We focus on the indirect categories that every dealership buys — and where most are overpaying.

Shop supplies

Gloves, rags, cleaners, fasteners, safety equipment — the everyday consumables that add up fast across your service department.

Oil & lubricants

Motor oil, transmission fluid, hydraulic fluid, and grease — high-volume categories where consolidated purchasing drives significant savings.

Parcel & shipping

Parts returns, inter-store transfers, and vendor shipments — we negotiate preferred rates with major carriers on your behalf.

Office supplies

Paper, printer supplies, breakroom essentials, and more — standardized contracts across brands you already use.

Uniforms & workwear

Service advisor and technician uniforms, including sourcing, embroidery, and laundry service contracts.
